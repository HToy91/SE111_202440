A REVIEW of Shameless

Jose Vargas Figueroa August 8th, 2024

8.5/10

Shameless is definitely not a show for everyone and might offend some people. Viewers should probably avoid having their younger children from watching the show due to it's mature nature. If they do watch it, you have been warned and should probably keep an eye on them. You should make sure they are not up to no good; stealing cash from your wallet, doing drugs, grand theft auto, vandalism, any criminal schemes, etc.

Every single episdoe of Shameless will leave you wanting more and you won't get by one episode without laughing. You will fall in love with every character, even one's not part of the main cast. The show is streamed on Netflix so grab your popcorn and get ready to do some binging!
