ALL ABOUT Shameless

Shameless is set in the city of Chicago and follows the Ghalager family. They are a dysfunctional bunch who do what they can to get by in the south side while their father, Frank, makes it harder to do so. Each of the Gallagher siblings struggles with their own issues and challenges while navigating poverty, crime, and dysfunctional relationships.

Release Date: January 9, 2011

Creators: Paul Abbott, John Wells

Production Companies: Bonanza Productions, John Wells Productions, Warner Bros. Television, Showtime Networks, Sterling Films (V)

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Certificate: TV-MA

Episodes: 134
